My Story

Situated on top of a hill, the town is 20 km away from beach and 100 km away from the western ghats. At the moment I work as a Full Stack Engineer at a Sports Media Company called Sportskeeda. I'm truly passionate about new technologies and am actively looking for new opportunities. The thing that excites me most, is building applications that will be used by people all around the world, helping them in their day to day activities, making life simpler and more accessible through the power of the internet.
